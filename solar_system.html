Sun

The life-giving star at the center of the solar system. Over the course of each loop, it undergoes a dramatic transformation, culminating in a supernova that resets the timeline.

Interesting Features:

  • A reminder of the impermanence of time and the cycles of the universe.
  • Holds the key to understanding the solar system’s biggest mysteries.

Ember Twin & Ash Twin

These twin planets are locked in an extraordinary gravitational bond. Ember Twin is a desolate desert world slowly being buried in sand, while Ash Twin is a barren rock being uncovered as sand flows between them in an endless cycle.

Interesting Features:

  • The sand "hourglass" effect is a natural marvel.
  • Nomai structures and mysteries lie hidden beneath the shifting sands.
  • A race against time to uncover the secrets before the sands bury them again.

Timber Hearth

A serene, forested planet and the home of the Hearthians. Its landscape is dotted with gentle hills, sprawling forests, and cozy Hearthian villages. Timber Hearth serves as the launch site for Outer Wilds Ventures and a hub of discovery for aspiring explorers.

Interesting Features:

  • Vast caves containing ancient Nomai ruins.
  • Geysers that can send explorers skyward.
  • A vibrant, welcoming atmosphere that embodies the Hearthian spirit.

Brittle Hollow

A visually stunning and geologically fascinating planet. Its icy surface conceals a web of caverns and crumbling ruins, all orbiting dangerously close to a black hole at its core.

Interesting Features:

  • The Hollow's Lantern, a volcanic moon that rains fiery destruction onto the planet.
  • Nomai observatories perched precariously on the edge of collapse.
  • A black hole capable of pulling explorers—and everything else—into its mysterious void.

Giant's Deep

A massive ocean world with raging cyclones and towering waves. It is home to strange islands and submerged Nomai technology hidden deep beneath the waves.

Interesting Features:

  • The electric cyclones are both mesmerizing and treacherous.
  • Floating islands that hold clues to ancient Nomai experiments.
  • A core hidden beneath the planet’s watery depths, protected by a deadly electric barrier.

Dark Bramble

A haunting and enigmatic world that was once a lush, fertile planet. Now, it is a tangled mass of thorny vines, mysterious glowing orbs, and pockets of treacherous void.

Interesting Features:

  • Dangerous and disorienting—navigating the Bramble is no easy feat.
  • Home to one of the most spine-chilling creatures in the solar system.
  • Key to understanding some of the solar system’s deepest mysteries.
