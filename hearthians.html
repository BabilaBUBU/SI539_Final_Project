Welcome to Hearthians' World

Welcome to the Hearthian Card Gallery, a curated collection of stories that celebrate the spirit of the Hearthian people. Here, you'll uncover tales of adventure, curiosity, and camaraderie, each told through vibrant cards that bring their journey to life. From the fearless explorers charting the cosmos to the warm campfires that keep their stories alive, every card offers a glimpse into the heart of what it means to be a Hearthian. Dive into the gallery, relive their most daring escapades, and discover the legacy of a species driven by wonder and a love for the unknown.

Let these stories inspire you to embrace your own sense of adventure, just as the Hearthians have done across the stars!

Tips: You may always flip the card of the Hearthians to see the other side for more information.

Hearthian Photo Gallery

Hearthian Exploration Team

Hearthian Exploration Team: A spirited group of adventurers from Timber Hearth, the Hearthian Exploration Team is the backbone of Outer Wilds Ventures. They are driven by curiosity and a desire to uncover the secrets of their solar system, crafting tools and spacecraft to explore the cosmos.

Hearthian's Spacecraft

Hearthian's Spacecraft: Ingeniously built from scrap materials, the Hearthian spacecraft is a testament to innovation and resourcefulness. With its quirky design and functionality, it enables fearless journeys across the stars, from Timber Hearth to the farthest reaches of the solar system.

Hearthian Astronomical Telescope

Hearthian Astronomical Telescope: A marvel of Hearthian engineering, the astronomical telescope stands proudly at the observatory. It allows the Hearthians to study distant planets, track celestial phenomena, and gain insights into the mysteries of their universe.

Heartians' Village

Hearthians' Village: Nestled among Timber Hearth's lush forests, the Hearthians' village is a cozy and lively community. Filled with laughter, storytelling, and the occasional campfire song, it is the heart of Hearthian life and culture.

Origin of Hearthians

Origin of Hearthians: The Hearthians trace their roots to Timber Hearth, where their unique culture and traditions developed. They are deeply connected to their home planet's natural beauty and to the shared spirit of exploration that binds them together.

Marshmallow is Always the Best

Marshmallow is Always the Best: No Hearthian journey is complete without roasted marshmallows. Whether around a campfire in the village or on a distant planet, marshmallows symbolize warmth, camaraderie, and the simple joys of life.

Museum

Museum: The Outer Wilds museum, located in the village, showcases the rich history of Hearthian exploration. Featuring artifacts, scientific discoveries, and Nomai relics, it is a treasure trove of knowledge for curious minds.

Deep Space Satellite

Deep Space Satellite: Orbiting the solar system, the Hearthians' Deep Space Satellite provides critical data about celestial phenomena. It plays a vital role in mapping the stars and monitoring distant planets for exploration.

Mistery in the Village

Mistery in the Village: A mysterious stone retrieved from the eerie depths of Dark Bramble, it rests near the village as a reminder of the dangers and wonders that lie beyond Timber Hearth.

Explorer's Camp

Explorer's Camp on Outer Space Planets: Scattered across the solar system, these camps are havens for Hearthian explorers. Equipped with supplies and cozy campfires, they provide a place for rest, reflection, and marshmallow roasting amidst the stars.
